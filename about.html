About Rome Stay Guide

Rome Stay Guide is an independent travel website focused on one destination: Rome. We publish area-based hotel recommendations to help travelers compare neighborhoods before booking.

Our editorial approach is straightforward: each page is organized by district, and every listing links directly to the relevant booking page so you can validate details, pricing, and room availability quickly.

We prioritize practical location guidance, clear language, and maintainable site structure so this resource stays useful as Rome inventory and traveler needs evolve.
